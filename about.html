“The mountains are calling...”

“...and I must go.” - John Muir

When I'm not at work, I try to spend as much time outdoors as I can. Depending on the day you might find me rock climbing, playing ultimate, paddleboarding, attempting to slackline, or jumping in the nearest river to escape the Texas heat. As you may have guessed from the quote above, I also love the mountains, and escape to them any chance I get.

Explore More

Travel is one of my greatest passions. My daydreams are filled with trips to the French alps, lunches on mountain peaks, and long drives with great friends. Every time I travel I get to experince something new, and learn more about the world. I'm always looking forward to seeing what's around the next corner.

Play Ultimate

The sport of ultimate has had the greatest impact on my life out of anything other than school or work for the past decade. Thanks to ultimate, I've made great friends, pushed myself mentally and physically to stay competitve, experienced the ups and downs of being part of a team, and had an excuse to travel to a number of places I may never have been otherwise. It has been the source of some of the most fun I've ever had, and will stay that way as long as I can keep playing.
